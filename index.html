Guide to my movies website

On the left of the page you can see a textfield with the add button down there,The text value which you enter there will be displayed as nav bar content.Initially it is empty.

On the right side of the page you can see a search bar on which the movies displayed will be according to the keywords you enter on the search bar.

In the middle you can see the respectives movies list. By default you can see the popular movies while entering the website.

Now, if you hover on the pics of the movies you can see the movie name, genre and rating of it and the text becomes bold if you hover on the respective text items.

If you click on the pictures, you will get the brief description,release date of the corresponding movie.

Also I added some animations on hovering the texts and pics, So explore and have fun!!
